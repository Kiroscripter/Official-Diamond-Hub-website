Diamond Hub

Diamond Hub is a powerful script created by Kiro, designed to enhance your Roblox experience. It currently supports two games: Forsaken and Die of Death, making it a versatile choice for players who want more control and exciting features.

Features

Stamina Automation – This feature ensures you never run out of stamina by automatically keeping it full. It removes the hassle of managing stamina so you can stay in action nonstop.
Automation – Handles repetitive gameplay tasks for you, such as farming, feeding, or grinding. This allows you to focus on progress and enjoyment instead of boring repetition.
AimBot – Helps you lock onto enemies with precision and speed. It significantly improves accuracy, making it easier to win battles against other players.
Skins [Beta] – Personalize your look with experimental skins. While still in development, it allows you to stand out and enjoy a unique style.
Settings & Optimization – Advanced controls let you adjust performance and visuals for your device. Optimization ensures smooth gameplay even on lower-end setups.
